- debian下载（本地包）
  - <https://cdimage.debian.org/debian-cd/current/amd64/iso-dvd>
- dd命令做安装盘
  - sudo dd if=/path/to/debian.iso of=/dev/sdX bs=4M status=progress oflag=sync
